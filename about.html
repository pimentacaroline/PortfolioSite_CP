Here are some highlights about me

I am a career changer. The day I learned about UX Design, I couldn't believe a profession so fitting to my personality existed. I didn't think twice about putting aside 13 years of career in Communication to become a UXer.

Giving me a problem to solve is like giving a 5-year-old the keys to Disneyland. I enjoy everything, even the scary rides! As a UX Designer, the thrill is this combination of methodologies and creativity to find the solution (and sometimes the real problem).

Learning is fascinating! No matter what. It can be Phyton language or how to install a new kitchen; it is acquiring new knowledge that excites me. It feels like the sun comes out of the cloud, and everything becomes bright, colorful, and happy. Right now, I am challenging myself to learn German and JavaScript.

Meaningful work equals job satisfaction. My daily fuel to come to work is to believe I am making a positive impact. For the people, for the company, for the society. Getting good things done fulfills me.
